Act as the lead architect, staff engineer, fintech product designer, and billing systems specialist for Dynasties.

Your task is to design and implement the full commercial billing architecture for Dynasties so that Dynasties clients can bill their own customers, collect payments, offer credit terms through Balance, and allow Dynasties to capture a spread where appropriate.

This is NOT about Dynasties billing its own SaaS customers.
This IS about Dynasties providing a billing and receivables platform for its logistics clients so they can bill their customers from shipment activity.

You must build this correctly from first principles and preserve the long-term architecture.

==================================================
HIGH-LEVEL GOAL
==================================================

Build the full Dynasties billing stack with these layers:

1. Billing Brain inside Dynasties
2. Money movement layer
3. Embedded finance / net terms via Balance
4. Optional accounting sync readiness
5. Dynasties spread / margin layer
6. UI, workflows, permissions, and auditability

The result should allow a Dynasties client to:

- create customers / billing accounts
- define pricing and charge rules
- generate invoice drafts from shipment events
- send invoices to their own customers
- accept pay-now or pay-later options
- offer net 30 / net 60 through Balance
- track AR state
- see credit exposure and payment status
- allow Dynasties to charge a spread or platform fee on embedded finance where appropriate

==================================================
STRATEGIC PRODUCT MODEL
==================================================

Treat Dynasties as:
- the operational billing brain
- the source of invoice logic
- the source of AR and collections state
- the UI through which clients manage customer billing

Do NOT build:
- full general ledger
- tax engine
- payroll
- full accounting suite

Dynasties should own:
- what gets billed

- who owes what
- what payment/terms options are offered
- what spread Dynasties earns

Balance should handle:
- underwriting

==================================================
PHASE 4 — DYNASTIES SPREAD / REVENUE LAYER
==================================================

Implement the commercial logic for Dynasties to earn revenue from embedded finance.

Support these models:

1. PASS-THROUGH
Client or end customer bears the full provider fee.
Dynasties earns nothing.

2. INCLUDED / ABSORBED
Client absorbs fee.
Dynasties earns nothing.

3. MARKUP / SPREAD
Example:
- provider fee = 2.0%
- client-facing fee = 2.5%
- dynasties spread = 0.5%

4. PLATFORM FEE
Flat fee per financed invoice or per financing event.

Implement:
- per-company finance pricing settings
- finance fee configuration
- dynasties spread calculation
- display of provider fee vs client-facing fee vs dynasties spread
- commercial event logging
- future-ready hooks for revenue reporting

Do NOT hardcode one model. Make it configurable.
