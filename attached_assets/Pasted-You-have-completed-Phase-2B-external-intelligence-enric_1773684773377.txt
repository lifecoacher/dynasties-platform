1. AUTOMATIC RE-ANALYSIS WHEN INTELLIGENCE CHANGES

Problem:
External intelligence is ingested, but already-existing shipments are not automatically re-analyzed when relevant intelligence changes.

Implement:
- change detection after intelligence ingestion/linking
- impacted shipment identification based on:
  - trade lane
  - origin/destination ports
  - linked entities
  - linked vessel/carrier where inferable
- durable reanalysis queue job(s)
- throttling / batching for large affected sets
- idempotent recommendation refresh behavior
- event logging for reanalysis triggers

Requirements:
- avoid duplicate re-analysis storms
- preserve tenant safety
- log why a shipment was selected for reanalysis

2. INTELLIGENCE SNAPSHOTS FOR AUDITABILITY

Create persistent snapshot models/tables for recommendation-time intelligence context.

At minimum implement:
- shipment_intelligence_snapshots
Optional if useful:
- lane_intelligence_snapshots

Each shipment snapshot should include:
- shipment_id
- company_id
- generated_at
- congestion_score
- disruption_score
- weather_risk_score
- sanctions_risk_score
- vessel_risk_score
- market_pressure_score
- linked_signal_ids
- external_reason_codes
- evidence_summary
- snapshot_hash/version

Link recommendation generation to a specific snapshot where applicable.

Goal:
Any recommendation should be historically explainable from the intelligence context present at the time it was generated.

3. PERSISTENT SCORING LAYER

Create persistent scoring models/services for:
- trade lanes
- ports
- carriers
- entities/counterparties

Implement deterministic score builders for each.

Suggested scores:
Trade lane:
- congestion score
- disruption score

- market pressure score

Port:
- congestion severity
- weather exposure
- disruption exposure
- operational volatility score

Carrier:
- performance score
- anomaly score
- reliability score
- lane stress exposure score

Entity:
- sanctions risk score
- denied-party match confidence

Persist scores with timestamps and update them on ingestion cycles or relevant reanalysis windows.
