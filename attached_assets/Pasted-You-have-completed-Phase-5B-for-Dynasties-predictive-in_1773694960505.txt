Goal:
Extend Dynasties from shipment-level and workflow-level operational intelligence into portfolio-level strategic intelligence that helps users optimize lanes, ports, carriers, counterparties, and margin/risk exposure across the network.

Do not redesign the existing intelligence and workflow architecture. Extend it cleanly into strategic views, scoring, recommendations, and analytics.

PRIORITIES

1. Lane strategy intelligence
2. Carrier allocation intelligence
3. Network optimization recommendations
4. Portfolio risk and margin views

1. LANE STRATEGY INTELLIGENCE

Create strategic lane analytics and scoring that evaluate:
- lane stress over time
- delay exposure
- disruption frequency
- congestion trend
- recommendation volume
- task/case volume
- claim/exceptions exposure where available
- margin pressure where inferable

Expose lane strategy outputs such as:
- stable
- monitor closely
- reduce exposure
- reroute under conditions
- reprice lane
- tighten booking gates

Add APIs and UI views for lane strategy rankings and lane-level strategic recommendations.

2. CARRIER ALLOCATION INTELLIGENCE

Create carrier strategy analysis that evaluates:
- reliability
- recommendation-trigger frequency
- switch-away frequency
- disruption exposure
- lane-specific carrier performance
- risk-adjusted performance

Expose outputs such as:
- preferred carrier on lane
- acceptable but monitor
- avoid under current conditions
- increase allocation
- reduce allocation

Add APIs and UI views for carrier comparison and allocation guidance.

3. NETWORK OPTIMIZATION RECOMMENDATIONS

Create a strategic recommendation service that works at:
- lane level
- carrier level
- port level
- entity / customer / counterparty level where appropriate

Examples:
- reduce traffic through a stressed port
- shift volume to lower-risk carrier
- apply tighter release gates on certain lanes/entities
- increase insurance posture on certain shipment classes
- pre-emptively create monitoring for certain disruptions

These recommendations should be deterministic, evidence-backed, auditable, and tied to portfolio signals.

4. PORTFOLIO RISK AND MARGIN VIEWS

Build portfolio views showing:
- active shipments by risk band
- predicted delay exposure
- predicted compliance exposure
- predicted margin at risk
- exposure by lane/port/carrier/entity
- mitigated vs unmitigated exposure
- trends over time

Add APIs and UI suitable for operational managers and executives.

5. SAVINGS / INTERVENTION ATTRIBUTION

Implement attribution logic to estimate and/or track:
- delays avoided
- margin protected
- risks mitigated
- interventions triggered
- interventions completed
- tasks auto-created
- booking holds that prevented issues
- comparative impact of internal-only vs intelligence-enriched recommendations

Make attribution assumptions explicit and deterministic.
